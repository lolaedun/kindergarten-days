Who is this for?

Are you a parent/carer and or a homeschooler? Are you looking for ideas to keep your pre-schooler or kindergartner learning in a playful manner? Do you like the idea of montessori and waldorf steinar based learning? If so, you are in the right place?

How does it work?

Once you register, you have immediate access to inspired activites for the current month. These are grouped into weeks and have been designed for the busy parent/grown-up in mind. They are quick snapshots to help you plan your week of play-based learning without feeling overwhelmed with large amounts of information to impart to your little human.

Kindergarten Days...

The life of the Kindergarten child should be largely filled with:

  • time spent outdoors
  • creative play with natural toys
  • modeling activities of the parent (helping them do dishes, cook, clean, care for siblings
  • teaching the older or younger siblings lessons

Your family schedule should always be the priority. You can choose to follow this schedule as little or as much as you would like. Follow the schedule exactly or skip some items. Let it be your guide and be there for you when you need it as a support system for you and your early childhood angel!
